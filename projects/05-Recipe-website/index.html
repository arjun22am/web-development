VadaPav - ₹30

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Garlic

Recipe

  1. Heat oil and temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, hing, green chilies, ginger, and garlic.
  2. Add turmeric, mashed potatoes, salt, coriander, and lemon juice. Mix well and form small balls.
  3. Prepare a thick batter with besan, turmeric, salt, baking soda, and water.
  4. Dip the potato balls in batter and deep-fry until golden brown.
  5. Slice pav buns, spread chutney, and place a hot vada inside.
  6. Serve with fried green chilies.

Kachori - ₹25

Ingredients

  • Kachoris
  • Curd (yogurt)
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Green chutney

Recipe

  1. Place 1–2 kachoris on a plate and lightly crush the tops.
  2. Pour whisked curd generously over the kachoris.
  3. Drizzle tamarind and green chutneys to taste.
  4. Sprinkle chaat masala, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt.
  5. Garnish with sev, chopped coriander, and pomegranate seeds.
  6. Serve immediately before they get soggy.

Masala Dhosa - ₹150

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for soaking and grinding)
  • Oil or ghee (for roasting)

Recipe

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter, mix, and ferment overnight. Add salt before making dosas.
  3. For masala, heat oil and splutter mustard seeds. Add curry leaves, hing, green chilies, and onions.
  4. Add turmeric, mashed potatoes, and salt. Mix well. Add coriander and lemon juice.
  5. Heat a tawa, pour a ladleful of batter, spread thin, and roast with a little oil.
  6. Place potato masala in the center, fold the dosa, and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
